Not Ashamed

Romans 1:16 is a powerful verse that reminds us of the importance of sharing the gospel of Christ with others. As believers, we should not be ashamed of the message of salvation, but rather, we should boldly proclaim it to the world around us. And that is exactly what we do here at Testimonies.

At Testimonies, we are passionate about sharing our stories with others. Our goal is to inspire and encourage those who may be going through tough times, and to offer hope and healing through the love of Jesus Christ. By sharing our experiences and how God has worked in our lives, we hope to show others that they are not alone in their struggles.

Share Your Story

At Testimonies, we offer a platform for people to share their stories of how God has helped them overcome. If you have a testimony to share, send us an email, and our team will work with you to create a well-written story. After you finish, we'll publish it on our website for others to read. For more information, visit our contact page.

TESTIMONIES.US

Testimonies is a place where people can share their struggles, or stories of how they were given a new life in Christ. This website aims to provide a space for individuals to relate, read real-life experiences, and grow in their relationship with the Lord. Furthermore, it is a platform for those who do not know God in a real and personal way, but desire to learn more about Him.

About Us

Who we are

Our goal...

  • To encourage all the believers and uplift spirits
  • To lead the lost to Christ
  • To witness to the whole world
  • To live for Christ in all that we do
  • To share our faith with others
  • To be a light to a lost and dying world

     Testimonies is a platform where people can share their life-changing stories for others to read and grow in their walk with the Lord. It is also meant for those who do not know God, so they can come to know the Lord and strengthen their faith in Him.
     Our primary goal at Testimonies is to encourage people who are going through tough times by sharing stories of others who have overcome similar challenges through God's help. Furthermore, we hope that those who do not know God as their personal Lord and Savior may come to know Him through the saving blood of Jesus Christ.
     Romans 1:16 says, "For I am not ashamed of the gospel of Christ: for it is the power of God unto salvation to every one that believeth; to the Jew first, and also to the Greek." We are not hesitant to share the gospel with everyone, and accept our responsibility to follow the Great Commission. Mark 16:15 states, "And he said unto them, Go ye into all the world, and preach the gospel to every creature."

Contribute

     Testimonies is a non-profit website that is dedicated to spreading the gospel globally. We believe that the gift of salvation is freely obtainable to everyone who accepts it, but we also recognize that spreading the message comes at a cost. That's why we rely on the generous contributions of individuals like you to help us distribute brochures, tracts, business cards, and other materials that can help us reach more people with the good news of Jesus Christ.
     Every donation, no matter how small, can make a difference in the lives of those who hear the message. Your support can help us expand our outreach efforts and touch the hearts of people who may not have otherwise had the opportunity to hear about the love of God. We are grateful for your willingness to partner with us in this important mission.
     Your donation will be used to fund the production and distribution of materials that will help us reach more people with the message of hope and salvation. Thank you for your support!
